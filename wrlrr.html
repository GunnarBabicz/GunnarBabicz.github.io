Wind Riders of the Lost River Range

Wind Riders of the Lost River Range was a group project lasting from August 2022 to December 2022. Our team was given a memo detailing a fictional business and their needs for a new, updated database. Our team planned and designed the database from scratch, including entity relations, queries, and stored procedures.

This project is still assigned to students at Idaho State University, so any finished code is unable to be shared. The original memo was published by Communications of the Association for Information Systems in 2005 and is viewable by the public.
